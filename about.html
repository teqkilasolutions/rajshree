Our Legacy

The Rajshree Story

For over three decades, Rajshree Jewellers has been synonymous with trust, tradition, and timeless elegance. Born in the heart of Jaipur, our journey is a testament to the enduring allure of Indian craftsmanship.

1985

The Beginning

Founded by Shri Ram Narayan in the bustling Johari Bazaar of Jaipur, starting as a modest workshop for gemstone cutting.

1995

First Flagship Store

Opened our first retail showroom, introducing hallmarked gold jewellery to a loyal local clientele.

2005

Global Footprint

Expanded operations internationally, exporting handcrafted Jadau and Polki sets to the Middle East and UK.

2015

Signature Collection

Launched our exclusive 'Royal Heritage' collection, reviving ancient techniques of enameling and Kundan setting.

2024

Digital Era

Embracing the future with our online boutique, bringing the Rajshree experience to patrons worldwide.
