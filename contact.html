Frequently Asked Questions

Do you provide certification?

Yes, every piece of jewellery at Rajshree comes with a hallmark certification for gold and IGI/GIA certification for diamonds and gemstones.

Can I customize a design?

Absolutely. We specialize in bespoke jewellery. You can book an appointment with our designers to create a piece that is uniquely yours.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 15-day exchange policy for unused items in their original packaging. Custom orders are not eligible for return but can be resized.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we provide insured international shipping. Shipping charges and customs duties vary by destination.
